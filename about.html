Update: Starting in July 2025, I will be an Associate Professor of Linguistics and Cognitive Science at Yale University. If you are a prospective graduate student or post-doc interested in working with me, contact me via email.

I'm an Associate Professor of Linguistics (tenure-track) at MIT. I co-direct the Language Acquisition Lab, where we conduct behavioral experiments to study how children acquire their first language. I also do theoretical research in syntax and semantics.

I am an Associate Editor for Linguistics and Philosophy.
