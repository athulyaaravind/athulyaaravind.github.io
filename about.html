I am an Associate Professor (on term) of Linguistics and Cognitive Science at Yale University. I direct the Child Language Development Lab, where we conduct behavioral experiments to learn how children acquire their first language. I also do theoretical research in syntax and semantics.

Before moving to Yale, I was a faculty member in the Department of Linguistics & Philosophy at MIT. I received my PhD in Linguistics from MIT.

I am an Associate Editor for Language Acquisition and Linguistics and Philosophy.
